This is a blue fish.

Blue fish have very sharp teeth and are one of my favort fish to ketch. They put up a great fight and are every where around the waters of Nantucket. The bluefish is the only extant species of the family Pomatomidae. It is a marine pelagic fish found around the world in temperate and subtropical waters, except for the northern Pacific Ocean. Bluefish are known as tailor in Australia and New Zealand, elf and shad in South Africa.
