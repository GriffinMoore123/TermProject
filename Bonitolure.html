1 = Albie Snax

Albie Snax are intended to mimic a variety of baitfish, including squid, sand eels, anchovies, and silversides. Bonito love this and once they bite on they give a fun fight.

2 = Little Mino

These lures act as a little mino. Bonito love this as it looks the exsact same as mino in a baitball. These lures are great as they come in many diffrent colors and very durable.
